Artificial Intelligence (AI) is no longer a futuristic concept—it is a present-day reality driving efficiency and innovation across the globe. For Tanzania, the opportunity is immense. As we look towards 2026 and beyond, AI stands to revolutionize key sectors of our economy.

## The Fintech Revolution

In Dar es Salaam and beyond, mobile money has already paved the way for financial inclusion. The next step is credit scoring using machine learning. By analyzing transaction patterns, AI can help lenders offer micro-loans to SMEs that previously lacked collateral.

## Agriculture: Precision Farming

Agriculture remains the backbone of our economy. AI-driven crop monitoring, powered by satellite imagery and local drone data, can help farmers predict yields, detect pests early, and optimize irrigation. This isn't just tech—it's food security.
